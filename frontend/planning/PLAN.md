# Plan: Refinement of Marketing Copy & AI Agency Concepts

## Objective
Evolve the Ignace.dev website from a jargon-heavy "Synthetic" concept to a clear, high-impact "AI-Augmented Agency" model. We will replace confusing terms with specific, valuable skills (LLM, RAG, MCP, MLOps, Agentic AI) and emphasize the Lead Architect + Agent Swarm workflow.

## Target Messaging
- **Lead Architect:** Expert strategic vision, quality control, architectural design.
- **Agent Swarm:** 10x execution speed, autonomous workflows, high-performance delivery.
- **Key Skills:** RAG Systems, MCP Servers, Agentic AI, MLOps, LLM Integration.

## Page-by-Page Improvement Strategy

### 1. Global / Navigation (`Navbar.astro`)
- **Action:** Ensure navigation labels are direct and professional.
- **Change:** Maintain "Projects", "Process", "About", but ensure "Start Project" CTA is prominent.

### 2. Home Page (`index.astro` & `Hero.astro`)
- **Confusing Terms to Remove:** "Human Ingenuity", "Synthetic Scale".
- **Proposed Changes:**
    - **Headline:** "Expert Strategy. AI-Powered Execution." or "High-Performance Systems. 10x Faster via AI Swarms."
    - **Sub-headline:** "Ignace.dev is an AI-Augmented Agency. We combine expert strategic architecture with specialized AI Agent Swarms to deliver production-ready LLM, RAG, and MCP solutions."
    - **Badges:** Ensure current badges (LLM Integration, RAG Systems, etc.) are styled for high visibility.

### 3. Services Page/Section (`Services.astro`)
- **Objective:** Ground services in specific technical value.
- **Proposed Changes:**
    - **Service 1:** "AI-Driven Application Development" (Architect + Builder Agents).
    - **Service 2:** "Custom AI Agent Swarms" (Autonomous workflow automation).
    - **Service 3:** "RAG & Knowledge Systems" (Internal data mastery).
    - **Service 4:** "MCP Server Development" (Extending AI capabilities with custom tools).

### 4. Process Page/Section (`Process.astro`)
- **Objective:** Explain the "Architect + Swarm" workflow simply.
- **Confusing Terms to Remove:** "Architectural Protocol", "Neural Analysis", "Structural Synthesis".
- **Phases:**
    - **Phase 01: Discovery.** Strategic mapping of AI requirements led by the Architect.
    - **Phase 02: Architecture.** Designing the system and agentic loops.
    - **Phase 03: Implementation.** The Agent Swarm builds the core logic at scale.
    - **Phase 04: MLOps & Launch.** Fine-tuning, deployment, and autonomous monitoring.

### 5. Projects Page/Section (`Projects.astro`)
- **Confusing Terms to Remove:** "Synthetic Deliverables".
- **Proposed Changes:**
    - **Title:** "High-Performance Solutions" or "Featured Work".
    - **Project Descriptions:** Update to highlight the use of RAG, Agents, or custom LLM features.
    - **Stack Labels:** Use clear tech labels (Astro, Svelte, Python, AWS, Bedrock).

### 6. About Page/Section (`About.astro`)
- **Objective:** Humanize the "Lead Architect" and demystify the "Agent Swarm".
- **Proposed Changes:**
    - Remove "Digital Twin" unless specifically referring to the Chatbot.
    - **Bio:** Focus on Ignacio's role as the human brain behind the AI machine. "Strategic lead for complex AI implementations."
    - **Core Foundations:** Replace "Digital Brutalism" and "Synaptic Architecture" with "Scalable Architecture" and "User-Centric AI".

## Verification & Testing
- **Copy Review:** Read all text aloud to ensure it sounds like a professional agency, not a sci-fi novel.
- **Visual Alignment:** Ensure the "Synthetix Noir" aesthetic (neon accents on dark glass) remains but supports the clearer text.
- **Links & CTAs:** Verify all "Start Project" and "Book Consultation" links are consistent.
