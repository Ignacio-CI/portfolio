## Problems & Pain Points
**Core problem:** Web development is either too slow (human-only) or lacks quality/depth (low-code/wrappers).
**Why alternatives fall short:**
- Standard agencies have high overhead and long lead times.
- Simple builders can't handle complex AI or cloud integrations.
- Hiring specialized talent for one-off projects is impossible.
**What it costs them:** Missed market opportunities, technical debt, inflated budgets.
**Emotional tension:** Need to look "future-ready" without the headache of managing a massive dev project.

## Competitive Landscape
**Direct:** Boutique Web Design/Dev Agencies — fall short because they are limited by human-only linear workflows.
**Secondary:** Low-code platforms (Webflow/Framer) — fall short when business logic or AI integration gets complex.
**Indirect:** In-house generalist devs — lack specialized AI-agent architecture knowledge.
